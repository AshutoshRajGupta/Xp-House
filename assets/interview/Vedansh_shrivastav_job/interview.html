2017-2021

Offers: Accolite Digital, Xoriant, TCS, Capgemini

  • The interview process of Accolite had 4 rounds: 1)Aptitude 2)Coding 3)Technical 4)HR
  • Aptitude: The test was taken on Eduthrill platform ( 90 questions 60 min ). Questions were a mix of technical, general aptitude, verbal, and reasoning.
  • Coding: The round contained a question that was based on Dynamic Programming. We also had to write test cases for the same
  • Technical: The interviewer was friendly but was trying to trap me at every question. I gave my introduction and he asked my favorite subject from engineering to which I replied HCI, not having much knowledge of the same he repeated the question and I replied SEPM. Again not having much knowledge of the domain he asked me the questions on DBMS asked me my introduction again! After hearing my answer he was impressed and talked with me in a very soft and impressed tone for a long time (1.15 hours approx.)
  • HR: The interview scheduled at 3:00 pm happened around 4:30 pm and the interviewer seemed tired and uninterested. He explained to me about the company and usual terms and conditions. The interview lasted for about 15 min only.
