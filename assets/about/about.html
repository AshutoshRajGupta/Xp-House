Overview

Today a student, tomorrow’s employee or entrepreneur. The journey isn’t going to be
easy, but a little ease can be added to it. Xp House is an integrated platform
that connects students, professors and the prosperous alumni network of different colleges
together. It is a one-stop destination where seniors share their job and internship
interview experiences and you can get all the updates about club events and
activities.
